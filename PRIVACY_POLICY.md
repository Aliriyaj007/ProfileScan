# Privacy Policy & Legal Notice

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-11

## 1. Privacy Policy

### 1.1 Data Collection
ProfileScan is a privacy-first application. We operate under a **local-first** principle:
- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit your search queries, IP address, or usage data to our servers.
- **Local Processing:** All search operations are performed locally on your device or via the local proxy server bundled with the application.
- **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not use analytics, tracking cookies, or advertising scripts.

### 1.2 Local Storage
This application uses your browser's Local Storage APIs (`localStorage`) solely for the purpose of saving your user preferences:
- Selected theme (e.g., Dark Mode)
- Platform selections and custom configurations
- "Legal Acceptance" state
This data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

### 1.3 External Links
When you click on search results, you are navigating to third-party websites (social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.
